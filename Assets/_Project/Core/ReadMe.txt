Welcome to Slingin Macros! a Hurricane VR project

Your goal is to sling food to hungry customers before they reach the bar in the main scene.

In the game you will open behind a bar and find several floating food items you can grab and throw. 
if the food makes contact with the customers they will be satisfied and dissappear from the scene.
